CHÀO MỪNG CÁC BẠN ĐẾN VỚI MY RESTAURENT

The architecture of San Fu Lou - house of three blessings is a perfect combination of contemporary design mixed with colors and materials of a traditional Cantonese house. Fresh lines intersect with traditional Asian motifs, highlighting the sophistication of modern culinary space design in the heart of Vietnam's busiest city.San Fu Lou is also considered as one of the first Chinese-style restaurants with an open kitchen design that allows diners to admire the process of preparing delicious dishes and a panoramic view of the skillful work of the chefs. All create a perfect, sophisticated and lively whole, standing out in the heart of the young city of Saigon named San Fu Lou restaurant.
